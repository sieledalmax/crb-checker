Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive my report?

Reports are typically delivered within 2-5 minutes after successful payment. In rare cases, it may take up to 30 minutes during peak hours.

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways. We don't store your payment details on our servers.

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your report, contact us within 7 days for a full refund.
